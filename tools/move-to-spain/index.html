How to use this tool
  1. Family tab first: Add yourself and everyone who's moving. Set each person's citizenship and visa pathway.
  2. Set your move date in the top-right corner. All deadlines calculate backwards from this date.
  3. Logistics tab: Answer the moving method questions to add logistics tasks to your timeline.
  4. Health tab: Fill in employment/insurance details per person to add health tasks.
  5. Timeline: Click the colored dots to mark items as done. Use the filter bar to view one person at a time.
  6. Everything saves locally in your browser. Nothing is sent to any server.
