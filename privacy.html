Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 4, 2026

This privacy policy covers the following Android apps published by Bellevue Tech:

  • Furniture Planner
  • Move to Spain
  • Spanish Tool
  • Task Tracker
  • Tax Todos
  • Trip Planner

Data Storage

All app data is stored locally on your device using the browser's built-in storage mechanisms (localStorage and IndexedDB). Your data never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to export or share it.

No Analytics or Tracking

These apps do not include any analytics, crash reporting, or tracking SDKs. No usage data is collected or transmitted.

No Ads

These apps contain no advertisements and no ad-related tracking.

Network Requests

Most of these apps operate entirely offline. The exceptions are:

  • Trip Planner loads map tiles from OpenStreetMap when you use the map view. OpenStreetMap's own privacy policy applies to those requests. Trip Planner also offers an optional Google Sheets integration that, if you choose to connect it, sends your trip data to your own Google account.

No other app makes any network requests.

Permissions

The apps request only the INTERNET permission, which is required by the Android WebView component. Apps that do not need network access make no network requests despite having this permission.

Children's Privacy

These apps are general-purpose tools not directed at children under 13. They do not knowingly collect any personal information from anyone.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a new date.
